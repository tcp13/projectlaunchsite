Project Launch Site

Welcome to the home of my weirdest side projects and half-baked ideas. My goal here is to launch small web projects that solve problems in unconventional ways. They're meant to be simple, pragmatic, and a bit whimsical. Though lean in implementation, these projects will prioritize user privacy, security, accessibility, and other best practices.

OptOutLetter.com

Stop junk mail by sending opt out requests! This free tool generates opt out letters that assert your rights by citing specific privacy laws and consumer protections.

WhyIsMyPoop.com

Poop is a treasure trove of health information! “Why is My Poop…” is a simple reference tool for understanding abnormal poop, including when to be concerned or ask a doctor.

WhereShouldIEat.today

"Where Should I Eat Today?" solved a personal problem of keeping track of my long list of favorite local restaurants. See at a quick glance which of your favorite restaurants are currently open.
