Project Launch Site

Welcome to the home of my half-baked ideas. Recently I've been a bit stuck trying to figure out what's next. This is my way of throwing shit against the wall to see what sticks.

My goal here is to rapidly launch several small web projects. I hope to explore some weird ideas, solve problems in unconventional ways, and incubate scalable prototypes with monetization potential.

While doing so, I'll stick to five values:

  • Simplicity: prioritizing core functionality within a narrow scope
  • Pragmatism: building for the end user's reality
  • Independence: bootstrapping without outside funding or external constraints
  • Sensible Design: optimizing accessibility, privacy, security, and other best practices
  • Whimsy: finding joy even in the mundane
