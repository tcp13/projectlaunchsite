Project Launch Site

Welcome to the home of my weirdest side projects and half-baked ideas. My goal here is to launch small web apps and micro-startups that solve problems in unconventional ways. They're meant to be simple, pragmatic, and even a bit whimsical. Crafted with care, these projects prioritize user privacy, accessibility, and other modern best practices.

TheGistNews.net

News for people who hate news. No Ads. No Popups. No Paywalls. No Opinions. No Speculation. No Breaking News. No Infinte Scrolling. Just facts!

WikiPOD.net

Print-on-Demand from Wikipedia. Get paperback books delivered by mail.

Haiku.ist

Powered by GPT-3, this simple web app uses AI to generate haiku poetry from any topic.

OptOutLetter.com

Stop junk mail by sending opt out requests! This free tool generates opt out letters that assert your rights by citing specific privacy laws and consumer protections.

WhyIsMyPoop.com

Poop is a treasure trove of health information! “Why is My Poop…” is a simple reference tool for understanding abnormal poop, including when to be concerned or ask a doctor.

WalrusPettingZoo.com

This silly clicker game puts you in control of a walrus petting zoo. Welcome guests, invest in upgrades, and fend off the occasional zombie!

WhereShouldIEat.today

"Where Should I Eat Today?" solved a personal problem of keeping track of my long list of favorite local restaurants. See at a quick glance which of your favorite restaurants are currently open.
